Shield Runewords[edit]

Runewords can only be made in regular items (ethereal or non-ethereal), and the socket count must be exact. They cannot be made in magic/rare/crafted/unique/set items.

Runeword changes include rune changes.

Ral • Ort • Tal

Required Level: 21

10% Damage Taken Gained as Mana when Hit 10% Damage Taken Gained as Mana when Hit

Ancient's Pledge is an introductory runeword and adds relatively little to the item beyond the +35% fire/lightning/poison resists gained from the runes themselves. Compare to 3 perfect diamonds, which grants +57% to all resistances.
